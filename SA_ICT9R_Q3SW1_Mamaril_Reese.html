Alice in Borderland


Synopsis:

In this two season series, we follow Arisu who gets thrown in a deserted Shibuya along with his friends, the only problem is, they are forced to fight for their lives in a bunch of life-threatening games while they try find their way back at the "real world." If they lose... I think you know the answer.

Ryohei Arisu

Arisu is the main character of the show. He is an unemployed middle-aged man who loves to play games. He is a selfless and strategic person for he wants everyone to live. He also has a group of friends, Chota and Karube. Their friendship, trust, and lives were tested in the games while all of them try to survive. He later on met Usagi who became someone important to him.

Usagi Yuzuha

Usagi is the daughter of a famous mountain-climber because of this, she was taught skills solely from climbers. She has a troubled past but tries to push it aside to survive. She is a skillful and athletic person, evading attacks from left and right in the games.

Chishiya Shuntaro

Chishiya is the smartest out of all. He's tactiful, intelligent, and observant. He had a past as a doctor just like his father. But even with his old job, he finds it hard to show a care in the world. He doesn't care for others, pretty much the opposite of Arisu in this aspect.

Kuina Hikari

Kuina is a transwoman who suffers her father's outlooks. Despite her attitude-like appearance, she can be a caring person for her friends and family, especially once we see a glimpse of her past about her sickly mother. She is a great fighter who has no fear to get up and fight again once she falls.

Morizono Aguni

Aguni is a muscular man who has a best-friend named Hatter. He doesn't much cares for others as he just wants to get the games over with, but when it comes to the people he cherishes, there's no doubt he wouldn't let that slide. He is another great fighter who knows when things are right or wrong.
